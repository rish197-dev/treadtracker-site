Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 22, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. TreadTracker does not collect, store, or share any personal information or health data externally. All data remains on your device unless you have enabled iCloud backup via your Apple ID.

Health Data

TreadTracker uses Apple HealthKit to access your walking and running distance. This data is used solely to calculate and display the mileage on your active shoes. We do not collect, transmit, or store this information outside of your device.

iCloud Sync

If enabled, your shoe mileage data is synced securely using Apple’s iCloud service, tied to your Apple ID. This data is never shared with the developer or third parties.

Third-Party Services

TreadTracker does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or external APIs.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns, you can contact us at treadtracker_support@icloud.com.
